Scope of works

Based in Yangon, Myanmar, BETIME Engineering has a proven track record as an M&E contractor involved in diverse projects, including factory and headquarters construction, handling fire fighting, plumbing, sanitary, and air compressor systems. Building on this foundation of reliability and technical expertise, BETIME now extends its commitment to quality by offering a comprehensive selection of high-quality spare parts for both cars and motorcycles. Whether you're a professional mechanic or a vehicle owner in Yangon undertaking maintenance or repairs, BETIME is your trusted local source for dependable parts, ensuring you can keep your vehicles running smoothly and get back on the road with confidence.
